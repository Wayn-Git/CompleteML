# Roadmap to Get Into Machine Learning

## Machine learning relies heavily on mathematical foundations. A practical breakdown of the math used in ML is roughly:
- 50% Probability & Statistics
- 35% Linear ALgebra
- 15 % Calculus

### Video Based Learning (Youtube):
- StatQuest – Statistics intuition
- 3Blue1Brown – Linear algebra playlist
- Khan Academy – Statistics, Linear Algebra, and Calculus
- Andrej Karpathy: Zero to Hero – Neural networks from scratch

## Topics to Learn

### **Core Probability**

- Random variables (discrete & continuous)
- Probability distributions: **Normal, Bernoulli, Binomial, Poisson, Exponential, Uniform**
- Joint, marginal, conditional probabilities
- Bayes’ theorem & real applications
- Expectation, variance, covariance, correlation
- Law of Large Numbers
- Central Limit Theorem (CLT)

---

### **Descriptive & Inferential Statistics**

- Mean, median, mode, variance, skewness, kurtosis
- Sampling methods & sampling bias
- Confidence intervals
- Hypothesis testing:
    - t-test, z-test
    - Chi-square test
    - ANOVA
- p-values vs practical significance
- Type I & Type II errors
- Statistical power

---

### **Regression & Model Diagnostics**

- Linear regression assumptions
- Ordinary Least Squares (OLS)
- Coefficient interpretation
- Multicollinearity (Variance Inflation Factor)
- Residual analysis
- Heteroskedasticity
- **Regularization**: L1 (Lasso), L2 (Ridge)
- Logistic regression basics & assumptions

---

### **ML-Relevant Probability & Stats**

- Maximum Likelihood Estimation (MLE)
- Maximum A Posteriori (MAP)
- Bias–variance tradeoff
- Overfitting vs underfitting
- Cross-validation
- Feature importance
- A/B testing basics
- Evaluation metrics: accuracy, precision, recall, F1, ROC-AUC

---

### **Light Bayesian Essentials**

- Prior, likelihood, posterior
- Conjugate priors
- Credible vs confidence intervals
- Bayesian interpretation of uncertainty

---

### **Advanced & Multivariate Topics**

- Covariance matrices
- PCA (as a statistical technique)
- Basics of time series:
    - Stationarity
    - ACF/PACF
